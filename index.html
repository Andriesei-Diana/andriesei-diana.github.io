About me

I'm Diana Andriesei, a Front-End developer with a passion for creating modern and intuitive websites. In February 2022, I stepped into the world of technology for the first time by taking an IT initiation course, and from that experience, a strong passion for web development was born.

In May of the same year, I continued to develop my skills by taking a Front-End Development course, where I learned the HTML, CSS, and JavaScript languages, as well as introductory notions about SEO.

In search of perfecting myself in this direction, I participated in a mentoring program in February 2023, where I was able to improve my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript languages, and adapt easily to the Bootstrap framework.

I am looking for an opportunity where I can put my skills into practice and contribute to the development of attractive and user-friendly websites. I am passionate about technology and willing to learn and adapt to any new technology that emerges in this dynamic field.

If you need a passionate and dedicated Front-End developer, I am here to turn your ideas into unforgettable web experiences.
